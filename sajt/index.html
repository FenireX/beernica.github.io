Services

We provide best quality food

Order

Customers can customize their order based on preferences. For craft beers, they may choose the type and quantity of each beer. In the coffee section, they can select their preferred coffee type and quantity. Any specific culinary preferences or dietary restrictions can also be communicated at this stage.Once satisfied with their selections, customers add items to their virtual shopping cart. The cart provides an overview of the chosen items, allowing customers to review and make adjustments before proceeding.

Shipping

Customers begin the process by placing an order through our user-friendly online platform. They can browse through our menu, make selections, and add items to their virtual shopping cart.During the checkout process, customers provide their delivery details, including the delivery address and any specific instructions for our delivery team. They also choose a preferred delivery date and time, ensuring flexibility and convenience.After successfully placing the order, customers receive a confirmation email or message containing their order number. This order number serves as a reference point for any inquiries or tracking.

Delivered

Upon delivery, customers receive their carefully packaged order along with a printed receipt. They are encouraged to check the items for accuracy and completeness.Customers are invited to confirm the quality and condition of each item. Craft beers are expected to be chilled to perfection, coffee selections freshly brewed, and culinary items prepared to satisfaction.With the order safely delivered, customers can now savor the flavors of our craft beers, coffee, and culinary delights in the comfort of their own homes. We hope each sip and bite brings joy and satisfaction.
